Cornation Chicken


Ingredients (serves 4-6 people)

  • 6 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 2-3 teaspoon mild curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoon mango chutney
  • 1-3 teaspoon sultanas
  • 500g of shredded cooked chicken
  • black pepper (for seasoning)

Instructions

  1. Mix the mayonnaise,curry powder,cinnamon,chutney and sultanas together and season with black pepper
  2. Add the shredded chicken and stir to coat in the sauce.Stir in 2 teaspoon of water to loosen if needed,then season and serve as desired
